With each passing day, the sickness of this planet becomes clearer to me.  The human race is a failed species.  Everyone is missing the point when they try to fix their problems or the problems of the world.  No one seems to see the underlying truth of the cause of all problems: we shouldn't exist in the first place.  This was a fucking accident.

People cannot be trusted.  They cannot be relied upon.  No one is truly for-another.  They are only for themselves, even when it appears otherwise.  They are disgusting, vile pieces of shit.

I want nothing to do with this species.  I separate myself from them as much as possible.  I was not meant for this carcass of a world.  

I was meant for another place and another time.

This entire reality should go up in flames.  I'd give my own life to ensure the annihilation of our species.  I'd suffer immeasurably for the rest of eternity to never let another draw a breath of air.

When the corruption of the human begins, there is no turning back.

The plug needs to be pulled.
